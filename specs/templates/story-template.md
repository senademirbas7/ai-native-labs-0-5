## INVEST Principles Validation Checklist

### Independence
- [ ] Story is independent and doesn't strongly depend on other stories
- [ ] Can be completed and delivered independently
- [ ] Dependencies are minimal and documented

### Negotiable
- [ ] Requirements are flexible and not overly rigid
- [ ] Details can be discussed with stakeholders/team
- [ ] Story doesn't prescribe implementation details

### Valuable
- [ ] Story delivers clear business or user value
- [ ] Outcome matters to the product and users
- [ ] Aligns with product goals

### Estimable
- [ ] Story has enough detail to estimate effort
- [ ] Team can understand what needs to be done
- [ ] No hidden complexity

### Small
- [ ] Story is appropriately sized for one sprint/iteration
- [ ] Can be completed by one person or small team
- [ ] Estimated at [X] story points (typically 1-13)

### Testable
- [ ] Acceptance criteria are clear and verifiable
- [ ] Story outcome can be easily tested
- [ ] Success is measurable
